# Candidate: Promotion Needs a Second Anchor

## Summary

This candidate proposes a stricter promotion rule: a judgment should usually touch at least two formal anchors, such as a source plus a project update, or two different formal pages, before it becomes its own insight.

## Provisional Notes

- The current insight about candidate notes being supplementary feels durable because it affected both retrieval behavior and project framing.
- A weaker judgment might look important inside one thread but still be too narrow to deserve a standalone insight.
- Requiring a second anchor could reduce premature page creation.

## Open Questions

- Is the rule actually better than a simpler `strong enough for reuse` judgment?
- Should the second anchor have to be a separate source, or can it be a second formal page that depends on the same conclusion?
